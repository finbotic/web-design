Deimos

The smaller of the two Exomartian Megacities, Deimos is even smaller than its brother, Phobos. Deimos, like Phobos, has had runaway population and industry growth. Deimos sports a population of 40 million.

In contrast to Phobos, the city of Deimos is one of the largest industrial hearths in the solar system - definitively the largest 0G / microgravity environment industrial centers, in both the inner and outer solar spheres.
